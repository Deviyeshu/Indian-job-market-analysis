# 📊 Indian Job Market Trend Analysis

> Analysis of 27,172 job postings to uncover demand trends, salary patterns, and fresher-friendly opportunities across 14 Indian cities.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective
As a recent MSc Statistics graduate actively looking for data roles, I wanted to answer questions that matter to every fresher:
- Which data roles are most in demand?
- Which cities hire the most?
- What skills do recruiters actually ask for?
- Which roles are accessible to freshers (0–2 years experience)?
- What salary can a fresher realistically expect?

---

## 📈 Key Findings
- **27,172** job postings analysed across **14 Indian cities**
- **SQL Analyst** is the most in-demand fresher-friendly role
- **Bangalore** leads hiring with 20.5% of all postings
- **49.4%** of all postings are fresher-friendly (0–2 years)
- **Data Scientist** has highest median salary at ₹18.5 LPA
- **R and SQL** are the top 2 most required skills

---

## 💡 Recommendations for Freshers
1. **Learn SQL and Python first** — they appear in the majority of job postings across all data roles
2. **Target Bangalore and Hyderabad** — they account for 30% of all data job postings combined
3. **Start as SQL Analyst or Business Analyst** — highest fresher-friendly openings with decent starting salary of ₹5.4 LPA
